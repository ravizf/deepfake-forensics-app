Deployment Setup

  1. Deploy the Flask app to Render.
  2. Set window.SNAPTRACE_API_BASE in config.js to your Render URL.
  3. Deploy this static page to Netlify.

Leave blank for local development. On Netlify, point this to your Render backend once and it will be remembered in your browser.
